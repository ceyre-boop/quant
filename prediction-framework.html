Semi-Efficient Market Prediction System

Markets are semi-efficient. Price already reflects known information. Prediction doesn't mean being smarter than the market — it means identifying conditions where an asset is statistically far from fair value, positioning is extreme, and a regime exists that gives the signal a statistical edge. All three must align. One signal alone is noise. Three together is edge.
